ABOUT

Hey There.

I'm Pranay Tummalapalli, an embedded software and control systems engineer with over 4 years of experience in robotics. I'm deeply passionate about robotic control systems, algorithmic control, and building reliable, real-world robotic systems.

My work spans a wide spectrum — from humanoid upper-body robotic limbs to robot-assisted laparoscopic and orthopaedic surgery platforms, and autonomous vehicle systems. I enjoy developing systems that combine precision, safety, and intelligence.

Outside of engineering, I am a classically trained tabla player. I'm also an avid motorcycle touring enthusiast — long rides on open highways give me a sense of freedom and clarity that fuels my creativity.
